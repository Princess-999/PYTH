About PYTH

Empowering the youth of Polokwane through technology education and digital literacy.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Polokwane Youth Tech Hub (PYTH) was born from a vision to bridge the digital divide in our community. We recognized that many young people in Polokwane lacked access to technology education and digital skills training.

Today, we've trained over 500 students in various technology disciplines, from basic computer literacy to advanced web development and programming.

Our Mission

To empower youth through technology education, providing them with the skills and opportunities needed to succeed in the digital economy.

Our Vision

A Polokwane where every young person has access to quality technology education and the opportunity to build a successful career in tech.
